VISION/About

CHCT INDIA aspires to contribute towards society by initiating training and development programs for poor and underprivilege sections of the society and developing opportunities to underprivileged sections of the Society. Raising Voice against any injustice irrespective of gender, caste, religion and race, providing a better future to them is one of our key Vision.

MISSION/What WE do:

At CHCT INDIA, we strive for meaningful change.We aim to build a society that empowers every individual to reshape their life. Our mission is to provide sustainable livelihoods to underprivileged section of the soceity.In other words, To enable people to take responsibility for the situation of the poor and underprivilege section of the society and also empowering them to help them in all possible ways. To provide a platform where youth from all over the nation unite to gather their knowledge and skills for a good cause.To work together to make drastic change in the lives of the underprivilege by providing proper health care, improving life standards and training them to excel in life.


WHO WE ARE

CHCT INDIA is a registered non-profit organisation based in Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh,that works in the field of community development, child welfare, education, Human Right Protection and Women Empowerment. It is founded by Mr.Ashok Tripaathi and Mrs. Saroj Shukhla on 28th Jan, 2016.
Our founders have faith in saying by Aesop, "No act of Kindness, no matter how small, is ever wasted."
At CHCT INDIA,We believe in bringing a smile on faces that deserve it the most but do not have access to happiness in their lives.
